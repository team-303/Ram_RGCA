AQMS

Aquatic Quarantine Facility for L.vennamei

Aquatic Quarantine Facility established during 2008 at Kapaleeswarar Nagar, Neelankarai, Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Major objective

To quarantine and ensure the SPF status of the SPF L. vannamei broodstock, imported by the CAA approved L. vannamei hatcheries from the approved broodstock centres located in other countries to India.

The SPF status is ensured for the absence of the seven OIE listed pathogens (White Spot Syndrome Virus (WSSV), Infectious Hypodermal Haematopoetic Necrotic Virus (IHHNV), Necrotizing Hepatopancreatic Proteo Bacterium (NHPB), Yellow Head Virus/Gill Associated Virus (YHV/GAV), Taura Syndrome Virus (TSV), Infectious Myonecrosis Virus (IMNV) and Acute Hepatopancreatic Necrotic Disease/Early Mortality Syndrome (AHPND/EMS) in the imported brooders, through the stringent disease screening protocols.

The facility is equipped with a quarantine infrastructure of 20 cubicles and operating in a high end bio-secure ambience, the facility can accommodate and quarantine maximum of 4,12,500 no.s of broodstock in a year.

Salient features of the facility

#1

A total of 1,35,000 nos. of brooders was received by AQF for quarantine & the mean quarantine survival achieved was 94 %.

#2

The facility upgraded the online quarantine space reservation system. Provision of emergency quarantine cubicles were also implemented in the online. User friendly features such as SMS and email alerts will sent to all AQMS registered hatcheries on cancellation of any cubicle online.

#3

Round the clock operation & provides year round services to the CAA approved vannamei hatcheries of the country

#4

Highly bio-secure facility with phase wise (In the month of April and September) shut down for a period of 30 days in a year

#5

Provide hassle free online service to the importers/ CAA approved hatchery operators

L. vannamei broodstock despatched from AQF since inception
