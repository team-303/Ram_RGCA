Short Decribe

Objectives /Mandates

Revival of Black Tiger Shrimp, P. monodon farming in our country after the gap of almost three decades through the consistent supply of disease-free high health seeds.

Diversification of Aquaculture in the west coast through the continues supply of quality seeds of mud crab, Seabass, GIFT and pompano produced at RGCA projects.

Introduction of GIFT Tilapia in large scale to support Inland fresh water aquacultures which would cater to domestic market.

Salient features of the facility

#1

Multispecies Aquaculture Complex is the first of its kind in the country and is an excellent adoptable model to other states of the country for sustainable diversified aquaculture

#2

It is the first 100% business model project of RGCA. The facility is totally running with its own income.

Achievements

Within a period of 10 months, the facility has supplied a total of 2.5 million fingerlings of GIFT, Seabass and Pompano to 1512 beneficiaries.

Supplied 2.35 million disease free Tiger shrimp seeds to 62 farmers.
