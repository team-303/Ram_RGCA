Marine Finfish Project

Marine Finfish Hatchery Project (MFHP) of RGCA was initiated during 2009 at Pozhiyoor, Thiruvananthapuram-District, Kerala. The MFHP has maintains a open sea cage farm at Muttom, Kanyakumari-District, Tamil Nadu since 2011.

Objectives /Mandates

Seed production and farming of marine fin fishes such as Cobia, Silver Pompano, Mangrove Red snapper, Giant Trevally, Tomato spotted Grouper etc.

To transfer the technology of seed production and farming to the aquafarmers for sustainable aquaculture.

To demonstrate open sea cage farming of economically important marine fin fishes and transfer of cage farming technology to the entrepreneurs.

Salient features of the facility

#1

The project devised and standardized broodstock Domestication, maturation and conditioning of Cobia, Silver pompano, Mangrove red snapper and Giant Trevally.

#2

The project carries out induced breeding and larvae rearing of Cobia from the year 2011 onwards and achieved a maximum survival rate of 15.5 % (from hatchling to Fingerling) against the world average 22.3 %.

#3

Conducting awareness programmes on the potential for farming of cobia and Pompano in sea cages as well as grow-out farms

#4

Conducting hands-on training programmes on “Open water cage farming and Cage fabrication” for the benefit of entrepreneurs.

Achievements

1.2 lakhs of cobia and 3.5 lakhs of pompano seeds were produced & supplied to 140 beneficiaries from various Institutes, grow-out farming demonstration programmes and aquafarmers.

81 MT of cobia and 3.4 MT of Pompano and 3.8 MT of other fishes were harvested from Cage project since inception.

This is the only project in the world which produces Silver pompano (long fin strain) entirely through natural spawning and we achieved a survival rate of 46.53% survival rate from Sac larvae to fingerlings.

The only facility which succeeds in achieving natural spawning of Giant trevally in the world

The project has been succeeded to produce Black Soldier Fly Pre-pupae for the first time and a total of 7.631 Kgs of BSF pre-pupae have been harvested and 6.4 Kgs had been provided as feed in Pompano Nursery rearing and RAS.

The project developed and standardized HAAT inoculum (inoculum of rotifers and copepods) for shrimp farms and supplied 2579 liters of HAAT inoculum in the year 2019-20 itself.
